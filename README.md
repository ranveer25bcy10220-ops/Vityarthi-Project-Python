# Vityarthi-Project-Python
## Train Ticket Booking System

 This is a simple Python project that books your train directly from your computer terminal. It is a neat  program where you can enter your info, pick your train and class, and get a ticket with a unique PNR number. In this project you can also feed your departure and final station. Great for learning or just having a bit of fun.

---

## Features

- You type your details and where you want to go.
- The system will pick a random berth and coach for you.
- It calculates according to your choice for different classes like AC, Sleeper, or Unreserved.
- Generates a unique PNR number for every booking.
- Provides clear, easy prompts so you won’t get lost.
